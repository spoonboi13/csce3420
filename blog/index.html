What my days look like.

January 1, 2021 by Spoonboi

I mostly work outside being a manager which is very fun. Some days it can be a lot with school but I manage my time wisely to make it all work.

First I make a cup of coffee at 7 am as I get ready for work. Then I go to my job, do my thing for about 8 hours, then go home, take some me time, then get to studying. If I have a lot of homework for the week, I make a to-do list so I can plan accordingly. After I do some homework for a couple hours, usually 4, then I relax with my partner before I have to do it again the next day. Someday's I just have school which is nice. I will mostly just work on assingments and take some time to relax and plan the rest of my week if I am not caught up. When I am caught up though, I like to hang with friends or go watch the sunset. Chess has been a fun activity to learn and really get your mind into learning new things.

Questions?

December 14, 2020 by Spoonboi

Going through school and don't know what to do with work or just questions on time management? Reach out and I will give you some tips. Or hey, you could even help me out. I don't know everything and I can definitely take advice on what could help me as well.

  • Remember to get lots of sleep. Drink your water!
  • Make a todo list, they help a lot.
  • If you are stuck on something, take a break, come back to it, or even sleep on it if you have the time and are ahead of your studies.
  • Remember to get out and make friends. Networking can help in a lot of aspects
